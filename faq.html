  • What makes this platform so different than other buy, sell, trade groups?
  • A

  • The first and only of its kind, revolutionizing the cosplay industry. Cosplay-Exchange is one place for all of America's cosplay listings to be found. You will no longer need to belong to multiple buy, sell and trade groups and sift through thousands of unclear ads that may not even be in our country. Every listing offers free shipping and is shipped within 72 hours. Our platform is for America’s cosplay community. It's clear, clean and direct with all transactions. It's honest and fair to the buyer and seller and even managed by cosplayers. Cosplay-Exchange transactions are done with e-coins (cosplay coins) so no more spending your paychecks on supplies and dedicating endless hours of construction time. You simply select your next cosplay and “exchange” your cosplay coins. The Cosplay Coin is the revolution to the buy, sell and trade cosplay community. In addition to Cosplay Coins we are now accepting all forms of electronic payment. As a seller you will receive your requested dollar price or Cosplay Coins (ecoins) when a purchase is made on the site. All members selling will make a subscription selection. There are two subscription options, pay per item sold on our platform or a monthly $8 subscription cost which charges no seller fees and unlimited listings. With the monthly subscription the seller receives15 free Cosplay Coins to use each month.

  • How does Cosplay-Exchange work?
  • A

  • It's very simple, you create an account, upload your old cosplays you wish to sell. Your uploads are then reviewed and approved by admin. Within the listing you will have the option to accept secure credit card payments and/or you can accept Cosplay Coins. The exchange rate for Cosplay Coins is one dollar for one coin Another member decides to purchase your item and sends an offer for purchase. You receive an instant text notification of the request. You have 72 hours to ship the item to the member. After the item is verified by the postal service as delivered your cosplay coins unlock or your funds are deposited into your account minus the seller fees if you do not have the monthly subscription. You pay $5 on every item sold without a monthly subscription but you also will receive 5 Cosplay Coins for your use in your member dashboard.
